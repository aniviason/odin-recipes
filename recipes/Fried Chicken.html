Fried Chicken

Description

Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces (500g) chicken leg meat
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tsp salt
  • dash of pepper
  • 4 tbsp wheat flour
  • vegetable oil (as needed)
  • weiners, hard boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Clean and remove fat and sinew from the chicken. Cut into large pieces. Dice garlic. Place chicken and (a) ingredients in a bowl and marinate 20-30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil over medium heat. Coat the chicken in flour and cook 4-5 minutes per side.
